Your Accommodation Route

You will stay at the HQ-booked hotel during Week 1 while attending offline training, then move into branch-prepared housing for Week 2.

Timeline:
Week 1: Stay at the HQ-booked hotel and attend offline training at the Training Center.

Week 2: Move into your branch-prepared housing, complete onboarding, visit your branch, and follow instructions from branch staff.

Apartment information: Your branch-prepared housing details will be provided before your departure for Korea.

Candidate Journey Overview

A simple step-by-step overview to help you understand what happens first, next, and later.

01 · Pre-Departure

Confirm your hotel and housing details

Confirm your flight, save key information, prepare your laptop and phone, and make sure you receive your branch-prepared housing information before departure.

02 · Arrival Day

Travel to the hotel

Land at Incheon, purchase a SIM if needed, then travel to the Premier Best Western Gangnam and complete hotel check-in.

03 · Week 1 Training

Attend offline training

Attend offline training at the Creverse Training Center and watch closely for emails from the Training Team.

04 · Week 2

Move-in & Branch Onboarding

Move into your branch-prepared housing, complete onboarding, visit your branch, and follow instructions from branch staff.

If anything changes, tell your recruiter or placement manager as early as possible.
Pre-Departure Checklist
Stage 01 · Pre-Departure
  • ✓Send your flight itinerary and receipt to your recruiter and placement manager for reimbursement purposes.
  • ✓Prepare a mobile device and laptop. A Windows laptop is recommended when possible.
  • ✓Download and set up KakaoTalk before departure.
  • ✓Check with your local bank that your credit and debit cards are authorized for international use.
  • ✓Download Naver Map and Papago Translator.
  • ✓Prepare enough Korean won cash for arrival-period expenses. Recommended arrival cash amount: 1,550,000 KRW.
  • ✓Make sure you receive and understand the Training Information Packet before training week. Check your spam/junk folder if you do not see it.
  • ✓Create your teacher’s profile at teachinkorea.com.
  • ✓Print this guide before leaving your home country and keep it with your travel documents as a backup.

Arrival Day at Incheon Airport
Stage 02 · Arrival Day
  • ✓Clear immigration and collect luggage.
  • ✓Purchase a prepaid SIM card at the airport if you need immediate mobile data.
  • ✓Choose transportation: taxi or Airport Bus 6703 to Eonju Station.
  • ✓Travel directly to Premier Best Western Gangnam Seoul.
  • ✓Check in as a Creverse instructor and notify your recruiter and placement manager if any issue occurs.
Recommended SIM purchase: Once you leave the airport, prepaid SIM cards may be harder to find. Purchasing at Incheon Airport is recommended if you need mobile data immediately.
Important taxi note: Use Orange / White / Silver taxis ONLY. Do not take Black Deluxe taxis. Late-night rides after 10 PM may cost more.
Prepaid SIM Cards: What to Know
Stage 02 · Arrival Day

Recommended if you need mobile data immediately after arriving at Incheon Airport. KT SIM cards are recommended because they are usually the cheapest option and service quality is good.

  • ✓Prepaid SIM cards usually offer unlimited data.
  • ✓You may not be able to make outgoing phone calls or send text messages.
  • ✓You can receive phone calls and text messages.
  • ✓Emergency calls work.
  • ✓Ask at the counter if you need an outgoing-call plan; it may cost more.

Hotel Checkout Protocol

Week 2: Branch-Prepared Housing & Branch Onboarding
Stage 04 · Week 2

During Week 2, you will move from the hotel into your branch-prepared housing and begin branch onboarding. There is no separate teacher-booked accommodation route for this guide.

StageWeek 2 instructions
Move into prepared housing Your branch will provide apartment information before your departure for Korea. Follow the move-in instructions provided by branch staff.
Branch onboarding Visit your assigned branch, complete onboarding steps, meet branch staff, and attend any required shadowing or setup activities.
Prepare before Week 2: Confirm your branch-prepared housing information, branch directions, move-in timing, onboarding schedule, daily expenses, medical exam fee, and ARC application fee.
